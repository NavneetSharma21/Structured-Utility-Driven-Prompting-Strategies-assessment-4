# Style Transfer

Formal → Casual

"Please submit the report by EOD."

↓

"Hey! Please send the report by the end of today."

---

Technical → Simple

Cloud computing lets you use software and files online instead of storing everything on your own computer.

---

Neutral → Promotional

Boost your productivity with our powerful project management platform.

---

Corporate → Gen Z

Big news! We're growing fast and bringing even more awesome opportunities.
